Upper case letters make your password more secure. Holding one of the shift keys on the keyboard will allow you to type capital letters. Alternatively, tapping the "Caps lock" key will activate capital letters. Tapping it again changes back to lower case.

Help

Lower case letters are non capital letters. If your keyboard is typing in capital letters, try tapping your "Caps lock" key to change back to lower case letters.

Numbers make your password more secure. The numbers can be found on the second top row of the keyboard, or on the number pad to the right of your keyboard.
